Overview

Decades of Experience

With offices in 10 cities, we are partners with more than 400 developers in 45 states, working hand-in-hand with them to close more than 1,300 properties containing over 76,000 affordable apartment units.

Today, we’re one of the Top 20 apartment owners in the United States. All told, we’ve invested more than $7.8 billion in affordable housing.

Enduring Partnerships

Building affordable housing is complex business. Our clients require an equity partner who understands the intricacies of these transactions. They also need a partner with the flexibility to close the deals. We offer both, which is why developers choose to work with us over and over again.

Experience With Every Type of Transaction

Over the years, we've invested in every type of affordable housing property, helping our partners finance their projects using all types of tax credits and debt structures. It's this extensive experience that allows us to provide developers with one of their most valued assets -- predictable closings.

Always in the Market

With $400+ million in equity raised annually, we always are on the lookout for investment opportunities.

An Involved, Responsive Team

As one of our potential partners, you’ll get to know our decision makers well.

Our Origination Team will serve as your advocate. Its six members will use their average of 17 years of LIHTC experience to fully understand your needs, working with you to negotiate investment contracts that work for you.

Our Underwriting Team will be your single point of contact to close your transaction. Its ten members average 12 years in LIHTC and perform comprehensive underwriting of investments. Our Underwriting team has the sophistication and experience to help you structure even the most complicated LIHTC.
