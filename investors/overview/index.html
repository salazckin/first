A Stellar Track Record

We have one of the most successful records in the tax credit industry. We’ve closed more than 70 institutional LIHTC funds since our founding, and we are adding to this total by sponsoring three to five new funds every year.

Our ability to raise capital is a direct result of our excellent returns. The actual IRRs of our funds average 106 percent of the original investment IRR.
